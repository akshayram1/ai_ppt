PROJECT INTRODUCTION: AI DXP

Project Overview

AI DXP (Digital Experience Platform), rebranded as Aeldris, transforms organizational workflows by simplifying AI integration. It makes unstructured information navigable and actionable through intelligent features.

Semantic Search

  • Understands meaning & context behind queries
  • Interprets complex & ambiguous requests
  • Finds answers, not just keyword matches
  • Maintains context across follow-up questions

Conversational Assistants

  • RAG-powered chatbots with contextual responses
  • Grounded in organization's actual data
  • Includes guardrails for accuracy & safety
  • Maintains conversation context seamlessly

Key Benefits

Streamlined AI Implementation
Better Information Access
Built-in Security Measures
Customizable Solutions
Improved Decision-Making
Cost Efficiency
